About

The Church of Nikita, also known as Anything Musical is commonly thought as a collection of talented musescorers/musicians. However, it is much more than that. It is a fiery lifestyle to glorify and spread the revolutionary figure, Nikita Ivanov.

Who is Nikita Ivanov? Nikita (/nɪkɪːtʰə/) is one of the many forms that the Minajor takes, transcending all mortal dimensions. He is the first Minajor Archon to surface from Minajorism. Nikita was first sighted by mortals on Earth in Siberia.
